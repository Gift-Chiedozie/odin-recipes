Ingredients:

  • 1 pack of spaghetti
  • 1 cup of diced carrots
  • 1 cup of red bell peppers
  • 1 cup of green pepper
  • 1½ cups of diced fresh tomatoes
  • 1 cup of already cooked goat meat or any protein of choice
  • 1 white onion, diced
  • 1 tablespoon of garlic
  • 1 tablespoon of ginger
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Black pepper, to taste
  • Seasoning cube, to taste
  • Salt, to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of curry
  • 1 tablespoon of thyme

Directions

  1. Put water into a pan and place on medium heat. Season with salt and a little oil and allow to boil.
  2. Add a pack of spaghetti into the boiling water and allow to cook for about 8 -10 minutes or till al dente (chewy, not completely soft).
  3. Drain the spaghetti out of the pasta water and run through cold water. Reserve a cup of this pasta water for later use.
  4. Heat up oil in a pan and sauté onions till it’s translucent.
  5. Add diced red bell peppers, carrots, green pepper, fresh tomatoes and goat meat
  6. Season with stock cubes, curry, thyme, black pepper, ginger powder, garlic powder and mix well. Allow to sizzle for a bit.
  7. Add the spaghetti into the mixed vegetable in bits and mix well to get it well incorporated till all the spaghetti has been mixed together. Allow to simmer on low heat for about 5 minutes or till done to taste.
