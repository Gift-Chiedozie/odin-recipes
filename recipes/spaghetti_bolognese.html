Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp of olive oil
  • 1 pack of spaghetti
  • Tomato and pepper mix (see below)
  • 1 satchet of tomato
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 cup of pasta water
  • 2 tablespoons of chopped onions
  • 1 teaspoon of chopped garlic
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)
  • 1 tablespoon of curry
  • 1 tablespoon of thyme
  • 1 teaspoon of black pepper
  • Seasoning cube, to taste
  • Salt to taste

Directions:

  1. For the stew base (pepper mix), you can either:

    - Blend your plum tomatoes, red bell peppers, 1 large onion, 3 scotch bonnet peppers, garlic and ginger. Then, boil the blended mixture on medium - high heat till the water is reduced and has thickened significantly to look like a paste; this should take about 20 minutes if placed on high heat.

    OR

    - Cut your tomatoes, peppers, onions, garlic and ginger. Season with a little olive oil and salt. Place in a pan and roast in a preheated oven of 350C for 20 minutes. Blend after

  2. Add water to the pot. Add salt and ½ tablespoon of olive oil then bring to a boil on medium heat.
  3. Add the pack of spaghetti to the boiling water and allow to cook for about 8 - 10 minutes.
  4. DAfter which, you sieve out the spaghetti from the water and reserve 1 cup of pasta water for later use.
  5. Heat up the remaining 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a pan. Sauté chopped onions and garlic until translucent.
  6. Add black pepper, curry and thyme and stir for about a minute.
  7. Add ground beef, stir well to combine and allow to cook for about 5 - 7 minutes.
  8. Check for seasoning and adjust with seasoning cube(s) if needed.

    Add a little more water (optional), if necessary and then add cheese. Stir well to combine. Allow to simmer for about 3 - 5 minutes then take off heat.

  9. You can decide to serve as spaghetti and bolognese sauce OR decide to pre-mix it in the pot before serving.
