Make your Own yoghurt Today!

  • 2 ltrs of milk
  • 1 cup of granulated sugar or honey
  • 3 teaspoons of blended cinamon, vanilla or any flavour of choice
  • 1½ tablespoon of yogurt with bacteria culture in it (e.g. Habib yogurt contains D.V.S culture)

Directions:

  1. heat up your pan to medium heat and pour in your milk
  2. Once hot, remove boiled milk from heat and allow to cool down until it's warm.
  3. Transfer warm milk into a glass bowl or container (don't use stainless steel because metals can interfere with the bacteria culture in the yogurt)

    NB: This draining process will take a couple of hours, so it better to transfer it into the fridge.

  4. Add in your 1½ tablespoons of yogurt with bacteria culture (e.g. Habib yogurt)
  5. Cover properly with a kitchen towel and keep in a warm place for 6 to 12 hours (or preferably overnight)
  6. Lastly, put the white firm custard looking yogurt into a cheesecloth (or clean t-shirt or scarf) with a bowl underneath. And allow the excess liquid (whey) to drain out of the yogurt.
  7. Once the yogurt reaches the consistency you're okay with
  8. Put it in a bowl, pour in your sugar, or honey. Then add the cinamon or other combination of flavours
  9. Stir and mix well, refrigerate for 3-6 hours, then it's ready to be served.
